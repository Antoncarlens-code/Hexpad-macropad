What it is

Hexpad is a compact clipboard / editing pad with a volume knob. The six keys wire straight to the XIAO RP2040's GPIO pins (no matrix, no diodes), and the EC11 rotary encoder handles volume. The 3D-printed plate, the PCB, and the firmware were all generated from one shared coordinate spec, so everything lines up exactly.
